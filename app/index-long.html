About us

Who is Solomon Strauss?

Solomon Strauss is a multi-award-winning performer who has been enchanting audiences with magic, mentalism, séances, and storytelling for over 25 years. Solomon Strauss has spent the better part of the past three decades weaving the threads of the bizarre and the extraordinary into every single performance, making those events a seamless blend of mystery and enchantment. Additionally, with over a decade of experience overseeing outdoor education and coordinating state-wide events for both West Virginia State Parks and Oklahoma State Parks, Solomon Strauss is well-versed in captivating and engaging audiences, no matter the setting.
